About Us

We’re a family-owned Italian restaurant serving Hawthorne since 1995. Our recipes are passed down through generations and made fresh daily. Whether you’re here for a casual night out or celebrating something special, we make you feel at home.

Every dish is made from scratch with fresh, high-quality ingredients — the same way Nonna used to cook. Whether you're dining in, picking up, or ordering for a celebration, we treat every guest like family.
